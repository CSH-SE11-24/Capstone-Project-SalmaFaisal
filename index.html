A Boogie with da Hoodie

A Boogie with da Hoodie, known as A Boogie, is an American rapper and songwriter. He gained fame with his debut mixtape, 'Artist', followed by the platinum-certified album 'Hoodie SZN'. A Boogie is known for his melodic style and hits such as "Drowning" and "Look Back at It".

A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie is of African American and Dominican descent. His mother is from the Dominican Republic, and his father is African American. He often incorporates his multicultural background into his music and lyrics.".
